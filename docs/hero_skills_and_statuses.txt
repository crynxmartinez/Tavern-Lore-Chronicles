================================================================================
                  MY TURN - HERO SKILLS & STATUSES (REFERENCE)
================================================================================

Purpose:
- Single place to look up Heroes, their cards (Attack / S1 / S2 / S3 / EX), and status effects.
- Intended for design + testing + multiplayer sanity checks.

Sources:
- Heroes: data/heroes.json
- Cards: data/cards.json
- Status icon/name text: scripts/components/hero.gd
- Default expire timings: scripts/battle/battle.gd (_get_buff_expire_on / _get_debuff_expire_on)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ROLE IDENTITY RULES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Tank:
- MUST have Taunt (on a skill or EX).
- Identity: protect allies by forcing enemy attacks onto themselves.
- Heroes: Squire, Makash, Stony, Gavran

Support:
- MUST have Heal (single target, all allies, or EX).
- Identity: keep the team alive, cleanse/buff.
- Heroes: Priest, Amihan (hybrid support/tank)

Scientist:
- Gameplay revolves around controlling your deck/hand or opponent's deck/hand.
- Identity: tempo, card advantage, equipment.
- Heroes: Dana

DPS:
- Deals damage mostly to single targets.
- Identity: high single-target burst.
- Heroes: Markswoman, Valen

Mage:
- Deals damage with AoE, usually tied to debuffs (thunder, break, etc.).
- Identity: AoE damage + debuff synergy.
- Heroes: Caelum, Raizel

Hybrid:
- Amihan is the first hybrid class (support/tank). Has both heal and taunt.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STATUS EFFECTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

BUFFS (active_buffs)

- shield
  - Effect: Absorbs damage before HP.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end

- empower
  - Effect: +50% damage dealt.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end

- taunt
  - Effect: Enemies must target this hero.
  - Default expire_on: opponent_turn_end

- regen
  - Effect: Delayed heal at turn start. Stacks.
  - Default expire_on: permanent

- block
  - Effect: Reduces incoming damage.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end

- bolster
  - Effect: Increased defense.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end

- star
  - Effect: Enhanced abilities.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end

- equipped
  - Effect: This hero has equipment attached.
  - Default expire_on: permanent

- dana_shield_draw
  - Effect: When Shield expires (consumed by damage or turn-end expiry), draw 1 card.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end
  - Note: Does NOT trigger on dispel.

- counter_50
  - Effect: Reflects 50% of damage back to the attacker (true damage, ignores DEF/shield).
  - Default expire_on: opponent_turn_end
  - Stacks with other counter_* buffs (each shows its own icon).
  - Only triggers on direct attacks (not delayed damage like thunder/burn).

- counter_100
  - Effect: Reflects 100% of damage back to the attacker (true damage, ignores DEF/shield).
  - Default expire_on: opponent_turn_end
  - Stacks with other counter_* buffs (each shows its own icon).
  - Only triggers on direct attacks (not delayed damage like thunder/burn).

- Note: Equipment reflect (e.g. Thorned Armor) is a separate system, shows bolster icon, not a counter buff.

- crescent_moon
  - Effect: At 4 stacks, consume all and fill EX gauge to max.
  - Default expire_on: permanent (no expiry, persists until consumed)
  - Stacks up to 4. Each cast of Crescent Moon adds 1 stack.
  - Card shuffles into deck (not discard) after use.

- eclipse_buff
  - Effect: Next EX this turn deals double damage (base + 100% base).
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end
  - Consumed when EX is used.

- redirect
  - Effect: 50% of damage taken is transferred to the hero who applied this buff (Kalasag).
  - Default expire_on: opponent_turn_end
  - Applied by Kalasag's Helmsman's Guard (SK1).
  - If the redirect target (Kalasag) is dead, redirect does nothing.

DEBUFFS (active_debuffs)

- stun
  - Effect: Cannot act next turn.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end

- weak
  - Effect: -50% damage dealt.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end

- burn
  - Effect: Takes damage at turn end.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end

- poison
  - Effect: Takes damage at turn start.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end

- bleed
  - Effect: Takes damage when acting.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end

- frost
  - Effect: +1 mana cost to all cards.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end

- chain
  - Effect: Cannot use skills.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end

- entangle
  - Effect: Cannot move or dodge.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end

- break
  - Effect: +50% damage taken.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end

- bomb
  - Effect: Explodes after duration.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end

- thunder
  - Effect: Lightning strikes at turn end. Stacks.
  - Expiration: handled separately (not removed by standard expire_on loops).

- marked
  - Effect: Takes increased damage.
  - Default expire_on: own_turn_end

- time_bomb
  - Effect: Detonates at end of opponent's turn: 10 flat damage + removes 1 random card of that hero from opponent's hand.
  - Default expire_on: opponent_turn_end
  - Applied by Nyra's Time Bomb (SK3).
