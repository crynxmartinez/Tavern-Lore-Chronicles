================================================================================
                            STAT SYSTEM
================================================================================

3 STATS: HP, ATK, DEF

HP (Hit Points):
  max_hp = base_hp × hp_multiplier
  Items increase base_hp → multiplied by hp_multiplier for effective HP
  When max HP increases, current HP stays the same (doesn't auto-fill)

ATK (Attack):
  Damage = ATK × card atk_multiplier × buff/debuff modifiers
  Example: 18 ATK × 2.0 multiplier = 36 damage
  Example: 18 ATK × 2.0 × 1.5 (Empower) = 54 damage

DEF (Defense):
  Damage reduction: every 1 DEF = 2% reduction, max 30%
  Formula: final_damage = max(1, raw_damage × (1 - min(DEF×2, 30) / 100))
  Example: 5 DEF = 10% reduction → 36 raw damage becomes 32

SCALING RULES:
  Damage cards  → scale off caster's ATK  (ATK × multiplier)
  Heal cards    → scale off caster's max HP (max_HP × hp_multiplier)
  Shield cards  → scale off caster's DEF   (base_shield + DEF × def_multiplier)
  DEF reduction → applied to target's incoming damage

================================================================================
                              BUFFS & DEBUFFS
================================================================================

TIMING RULE:
  Buffs/debuffs use expire_on system:
    own_turn_end     → removed when the affected hero's owner ends their turn
    opponent_turn_end → removed when the opponent ends their turn
    permanent        → never auto-expires (consumed by mechanic or removed manually)
  Shield (block) expires at end of opponent's turn or when absorbed by damage.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
BUFFS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Empower:   +50% damage dealt, +50% heal, +50% shield. Until end of own turn.
Taunt:     Enemies must target this hero. Until end of opponent's turn.
Regen:     Delayed heal at turn start. Stacks. Consumed after healing. (permanent)
             Example: Regen 10 + Regen 10 = heals 20 at next turn start, then removed.
Bolster:   Indicates equipment is attached (passive, permanent).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
DEBUFFS (Status) — Active during owner's turn, cleared at end
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Weak:      -50% damage dealt, -50% heal, -50% shield. (Opposite of Empower)
Break:     +50% damage taken, -50% shield and heal received.
Stun:      Cannot act.
Frost:     +1 mana cost to all cards from the frosted hero.
Chain:     Cannot use EX skill.
Entangle:  +5 flat damage taken. Does not stack.
             Interaction: Break (+50%) applies first, then +5 flat.
Target:    Taunt-like debuff — forces enemies to focus this hero.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
DEBUFFS (Stackable / Detonation) — Trigger at end of owner's turn
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Thunder:   Stacks × base damage. Consumes stacks on trigger.
Bomb:      20 flat damage to ALL enemies. Stackable. Consumes on trigger.
Decay:     Stacks × base damage. BYPASSES SHIELD. Consumes on trigger.
Star:      Stacks × base damage. Consumes stacks on trigger.
Fire:      Damage dealt becomes DoT. Stackable. PERMANENT until removed.
             Ticks at end of owner's turn. Does NOT auto-clear.
