================================================================================
                         MY TURN - EQUIPMENT SYSTEM
================================================================================

OVERVIEW:
  - 10 unique equipment cards available in the game
  - Player selects 4 equipment cards to add to their deck before battle
  - Each hero can equip 1 equipment at a time
  - Equipment provides passive auto-skills (no stat boosts)
  - Auto-skills trigger automatically when conditions are met

================================================================================
                            EQUIPMENT RULES
================================================================================

DECK BUILDING:
  - Choose 4 equipment cards from the 10 available
  - Equipment cards are shuffled into your deck with hero cards

EQUIPPING:
  - Play equipment card by targeting an ally hero
  - Costs mana to equip (varies per equipment)
  - Hero can only hold 1 equipment at a time
  - Cannot equip if hero already has equipment

EQUIPMENT PERSISTENCE:
  - Equipment stays on hero until hero dies
  - When hero dies: equipment goes to DISCARD PILE
  - If hero is revived: they have NO equipment (can equip new one)
  - Discard pile reshuffles into deck when deck is empty

CANNOT:
  - Move equipment between heroes
  - Stack multiple equipment on one hero
  - Unequip voluntarily (only lost on death)

================================================================================
                          EQUIPMENT CARDS (10)
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. VAMPIRIC BLADE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Mana Cost: 1
   Trigger: On dealing damage
   Effect: Heal 10% of damage dealt (minimum 1 HP)
   
   Best For: DPS heroes who attack frequently
   Strategy: Sustain through combat without needing healer

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. THORNED ARMOR
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Mana Cost: 1
   Trigger: On taking damage
   Effect: Reflect 20% damage back to attacker (minimum 1)
   
   Best For: Tanks with Taunt who take lots of hits
   Strategy: Punish enemies for attacking your tank

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. PHOENIX FEATHER
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Mana Cost: 2
   Trigger: On death (once per battle)
   Effect: Immediately revive with 30% max HP
   Note: After triggering, equipment is consumed (not sent to discard)
   
   Best For: Key heroes you can't afford to lose
   Strategy: Insurance for your carry or healer

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. BERSERKER'S AXE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Mana Cost: 1
   Trigger: When HP drops below 50% (once per equip)
   Effect: Gain Empower buff
   
   Best For: DPS heroes who can survive at low HP
   Strategy: Risk/reward - more damage when in danger

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. GUARDIAN'S SHIELD
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Mana Cost: 2
   Trigger: When an adjacent ally takes damage
   Effect: Reduce that damage by 5
   
   Best For: Tanks positioned next to squishy allies
   Strategy: Protect your backline from AoE damage
   Note: "Adjacent" = hero in position directly next to this hero

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. MANA SIPHON
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Mana Cost: 0
   Trigger: On killing an enemy
   Effect: Gain +1 mana this turn
   
   Best For: Heroes who secure kills
   Strategy: Snowball advantage by chaining kills into more plays

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7. ENERGY PENDANT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Mana Cost: 1
   Trigger: On dealing damage
   Effect: Gain +5 extra energy (EX gauge)
   
   Best For: Heroes with powerful EX skills
   Strategy: Rush to EX skill faster for big plays

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8. CLEANSING CHARM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Mana Cost: 1
   Trigger: On turn start
   Effect: Remove 1 debuff from this hero
   
   Best For: Key heroes vulnerable to stun/weak
   Strategy: Counter debuff-heavy enemy teams

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
9. FROST GAUNTLET
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Mana Cost: 1
   Trigger: On dealing damage
   Effect: Apply Weak to the target
   
   Best For: Heroes who attack frequently or hit multiple targets
   Strategy: Reduce enemy damage output through constant Weak application

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
10. BATTLE HORN
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Mana Cost: 2
   Trigger: On killing an enemy
   Effect: Empower ALL allies
   
   Best For: Heroes who secure kills
   Strategy: Turn kills into team-wide damage boost

================================================================================
                         EQUIPMENT MANA COST SUMMARY
================================================================================

   0 Mana: Mana Siphon
   1 Mana: Vampiric Blade, Thorned Armor, Berserker's Axe, 
           Energy Pendant, Cleansing Charm, Frost Gauntlet
   2 Mana: Phoenix Feather, Guardian's Shield, Battle Horn

================================================================================
                            DESIGN NOTES
================================================================================

BALANCE PHILOSOPHY:
  - Higher impact effects = higher mana cost
  - "On kill" effects are powerful but conditional
  - "On damage" effects are consistent but weaker per trigger
  - "On death" effects are insurance (you hope not to use them)

SYNERGIES TO CONSIDER:
  - Vampiric Blade + Markswoman (high damage = high sustain)
  - Thorned Armor + Stony with Taunt (reflect while tanking)
  - Phoenix Feather + Priest (backup if healer dies)
  - Berserker's Axe + Tank (low HP but high shield)
  - Guardian's Shield + Front Tank (protect position 2-4)
  - Mana Siphon + DPS (kill = more cards = more kills)
  - Energy Pendant + Makash (rush to Fallen Strike)
  - Cleansing Charm + Any (anti-stun insurance)
  - Frost Gauntlet + AoE attacker (Weak entire enemy team)
  - Battle Horn + Assassin/DPS (team Empower on kills)
